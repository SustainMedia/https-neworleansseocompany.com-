Sustain Media’s Dental SEO Services

Bring in More Patients and Grow Your Practice

Dental SEO Audit

We start with a complete review of your current SEO performance. From your website’s structure to your Google Business Profile, we evaluate every factor that impacts your online visibility. Our team checks for issues like missing keywords, slow page speed, and incomplete listings. This thorough audit helps us create a roadmap to improve your search rankings and attract new patients.

Keyword Research & Strategy

We identify the exact phrases patients in your area use when searching for dental services. Whether it’s "emergency dentist near me" or "Invisalign in [city]," we focus on high-intent keywords that bring in new patients. These insights guide our strategy, helping your website rank for the terms that matter most to your practice.

On-Page SEO

We optimize every page of your website to ensure it ranks well in search results. This includes refining your service pages, updating meta descriptions and headers, and adding location-based keywords to help patients in your area find you. We also improve your site’s speed and usability, making it easier for visitors to navigate and schedule appointments.

Google Business Profile Optimization

We help you get the most out of your Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business). Our team ensures your profile is complete, accurate, and optimized for local searches. From managing patient reviews to adding updated photos, we make your practice stand out in local search results and on Google Maps.

Content Marketing for Dentists

We create informative and engaging content that positions your practice as a trusted authority. From blogs about dental hygiene tips to FAQs about treatments, our content not only attracts new visitors but also helps convert them into patients. Every piece is optimized for SEO, driving more traffic to your site and building trust with potential patients.

Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)

We help turn your website visitors into booked appointments. By improving your site’s layout, simplifying forms, and making your contact information easy to find, we increase the likelihood of patients choosing your practice. Small adjustments lead to big improvements in conversions.

Technical SEO

Our team handles the technical aspects of SEO, such as fixing broken links, improving site speed, and ensuring your site is mobile-friendly. These improvements enhance the user experience and make your site more appealing to search engines.

Local Citation Management

We ensure your dental practice’s information is accurate across all online directories, such as Google, Yelp, and Healthgrades. Consistent and correct listings build trust with search engines and patients, helping you rank higher in local searches.

Reputation Management

Patient reviews play a big role in choosing a dentist. We help you manage your online reputation by responding to reviews, encouraging positive feedback, and addressing any issues that arise. A strong online reputation builds trust and encourages more patients to choose your practice.
